ScientificState is a research infrastructure tool. It structures, tracks, and manages
scientific claims and their epistemic state. It does **not**:

- Replace peer review, institutional review boards, or regulatory evaluation

## Computational Method Resolution

The Computational Method Resolution Engine (CMRE) is a constraint solver that identifies
which analytical methods are valid or invalid under declared assumptions. It does **not**
recommend, rank, or select methods. The researcher is solely responsible for method
selection and the interpretation of results.
